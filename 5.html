Harvard Business Review made the following recommendations for getting started with AI: Apply AI capabilities to activities that have the most and immediate impact on revenue and cost. Use AI to boost productivity with the same number of people, rather than removing or adding employees. Start implementing AI in the back office, not the front office (you will benefit greatly from applying it to IT and accounting).
